# IDIYANALE NPK SENSOR 
The NPK Soil Sensor is designed to measure the levels of Nitrogen (N), Phosphorus (P), and Potassium (K) present in the soil. These nutrients are vital for plant growth and development, making the sensor useful in agriculture, gardening, and smart farming applications. It enables farmers and researchers to assess soil fertility and identify the appropriate fertilizer requirements for crops.

The system integrates an NPK sensor with an Arduino Uno to gather and display real-time soil nutrient data. Through continuous monitoring of nutrient levels, users can make informed decisions regarding fertilizer application, helping minimize waste while improving crop health, quality, and productivity.

This project demonstrates the use of modern technology in agriculture by offering a more efficient, accurate, and convenient approach to soil analysis and nutrient management. Its goal is to assist farmers and gardeners in monitoring soil conditions effectively and making better decisions to enhance crop growth and overall agricultural performance.
